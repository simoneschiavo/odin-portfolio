About Me

I’m Patrick Jane, consultant for the California Bureau of Investigation. I solve crimes using observation, intuition, and a deep understanding of human nature—no badge, no gun, just my mind. Once, I was a fake psychic, conning people for a living. Now, I use those same skills to uncover the truth and bring justice, a small way of atoning for my past. I see what others miss—the lies, the tells, the hidden stories. If you’re hiding something, don’t bother. I’ll know.

My 7 Final Red John Suspects

Bret Stiles

He’s a charming devil cloaked in enlightenment. A self-proclaimed guru, master manipulator, and head of the Visualize cult—a man who can turn a lie into a scripture with just a smile. He’s clever, dangerous, and always playing a long game. Don’t trust him, but admire the show.

Gale Bertram

He’s the bureaucrat’s bureaucrat—polished, calculated, and always saying just enough to keep you guessing. The kind of man who shakes your hand while hiding a knife behind his back. Trust him? Only if you enjoy surprises—and not the good kind.

Ray Haffner

The poster boy for ambition and obedience. Always impeccably dressed, always by the book, and always looking for the next rung on the ladder. A bit stiff, a bit smug, and far too concerned with appearances. He’s the kind of guy who’d sell you sincerity—if it came with a promotion.

Reede Smith

He’s a blunt instrument in a suit—big, tough, and not exactly subtle. The kind of guy who’d break down a door when there’s a perfectly good key. Loyal, but easily led, especially by those who know how to push the right buttons. A follower pretending he’s in charge.

Robert Kirkland

Calm, calculated, and shrouded in just enough mystery to make you nervous. A man with secrets tucked neatly behind a charming, disarming demeanor. He plays his cards close to the chest, but trust me, he’s always got a hidden hand.

Thomas McAllister

The small-town sheriff with a quiet smile and an unassuming manner. Harmless at first glance—almost forgettable. But don’t let that fool you. Still waters run deep, and some of them are darker than they look.

Brett Partridge

He is… peculiar. Obsessed with death, forensics, and the macabre, he’s the kind of guy who finds joy in the gory details. Enthusiastic but unnervingly so, like a kid who never quite learned what’s appropriate at a dinner table.
